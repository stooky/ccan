**Priority:** P3
**Effort:** L (4-8 hours)
**Status:** ready

## Description
Expand the existing referral program with more visibility and potentially a loyalty component. BigSteelBox has AIR MILES integration. A strong referral program drives word-of-mouth and repeat business.

## Current State
- Referral page exists at `/referrals`
- Basic referral offer in place

## Enhancement Ideas

### Referral Program Improvements
1. **Clearer incentive structure**
   - Referrer gets: $X or % off next purchase
   - Referee gets: $X off first purchase

2. **Tracking system**
   - Unique referral codes
   - Dashboard for referrers to track status

3. **Tiered rewards**
   - 1 referral: $50 credit
   - 3 referrals: $200 credit
   - 5 referrals: Free delivery on next order

### Loyalty Program (New)
1. **Repeat customer benefits**
   - Returning customer discount
   - Priority scheduling
   - Exclusive offers

2. **Business accounts**
   - Volume pricing
   - Net-30 terms
   - Dedicated support

## Technical Implementation

### Referral Tracking
- Generate unique codes per customer
- Track in database/JSON
- Admin dashboard for management

### Integration Points
- Quote form: "Referral code" field
- Email: Referral program mention
- Thank you page: "Refer a friend" CTA
