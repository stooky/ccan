Executive Summary

Over 15 months, ccansam.com transformed from a low-visibility site buried on page 3–4 of Google into a page-1 competitor across Saskatchewan's shipping container market. A full site rebuild completed around October 2025 caused a short-term dip in Google impressions, but the new structure — city-specific pages, optimized URLs, guide content — compounded into the best organic performance in the site's history by March 2026.

The bottom line: Google search clicks grew +123%, average position improved from page 3 to page 1, and the site is now visible in AI search engines for the first time.
